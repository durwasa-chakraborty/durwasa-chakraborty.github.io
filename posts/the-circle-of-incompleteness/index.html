The Circle of Incompleteness

May 30, 2023 · durwasa

Am I too comfortable or am I dying from within
I wish at the word go I could drop my skin
The quotes of the dead do not interest me
Nor does the aphorism of the sickened living

I am still searching for lost letters and their meaning
To go back to the gardens of my uninterrupted musing
And every night I hope to find the old you;
And every day I hope for a new story to begin.
